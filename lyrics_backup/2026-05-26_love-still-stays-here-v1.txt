[Hook]
I feel the heat when you pull me close
A quiet spark that only midnight knows
The world can fade into the velvet blue
I only want to spend this life with you

[Verse 1]
The firelight dances on the cabin wall
A whispered secret as the shadows fall
An old guitar is resting by the bed
I hang on every tender word you said

[Pre-Chorus]
A slow burn rising in the candle glow
A sweet surrender that we take so slow
Every breath is pulling down the line
I am forever yours, and you are mine

[Chorus]
Love still stays here, safe inside my arms
Far away from all the world's alarms
I want to hold you till the morning light
And keep you wrapped up in this tender night
